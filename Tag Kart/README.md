## 📱 Interactive Modules

### 1️⃣ Login Page
Focused on the core navigation and the complex notification architecture.
*   **Visuals:** Login Button, Glass morph design and Matching Color Palates.
*   [**🔗 Open Figma Project**](https://www.figma.com/design/7A1nXT8lHmcRkIBlG6LLur/Tag-Kart-Login?node-id=0-1&t=Y8IhsLc2NX597ADY-1)

### 2️⃣ App AD Design
An exploration of color theory and interface adaptability.
*   **Visuals:** Shop Now, Limited Offers and Add to Cart Button with user interactive design.
*   **Logic:** Tap product image to view products image with smooth animations effects.
*   [**🔗 Open Figma Project**](https://www.figma.com/design/dVxZrbKk9RmJl40NsEeGcy/Tag-Kart-1?node-id=0-1&t=gqiwSiBCBlAaq6dy-1)

### 2️⃣ Brochures
Focuses on taking reader's Attention
*   **Visuals:** Matching the App Design patterns and Containd Offers and other Informations.
*   [**🔗 Open Figma Project**](https://www.figma.com/design/8MnJ3e0H9IPG6445AeJatM/UIUXCSD27-Practical-3?node-id=0-1&t=SzliTbPsAnAm3Iry-1)

## 🛠️ Technical Details
*   **Software:** Figma (Pen Tool, Boolean Operations, Corner Smoothing).
*   **Assets:** 100% original vector work.
*   **Backgrounds:** Product Images sourced from Google Images for realistic context.
